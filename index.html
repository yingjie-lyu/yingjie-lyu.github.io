Biography

I am a Master's student at Shandong University, advised by Zengpeng Li. Previously, I got my Bachelor's degree from Shandong University in 2023.

My research interests focus on cryptographic primitives and protocols, including digital signatures, secure multi-party computation, and threshold cryptography. In particular, I have worked on the design and analysis of some threshold ECDSA schemes.

I expect to graduate in June 2026, and I am currently looking for a Ph.D. position starting in Fall 2026.
