Biography

I recently received my Master's degree from Shandong University, where I was advised by Zengpeng Li. Previously, I obtained my Bachelor's degree from Shandong University in 2023.

My research interests focus on cryptographic primitives and protocols, including digital signatures, secure multi-party computation, and threshold cryptography. In particular, I have worked on the design and analysis of some threshold ECDSA schemes.

I am currently looking for a Ph.D. position starting in Fall 2026.
